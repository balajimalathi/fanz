### 1. Subscription Cancellation
You can cancel your RepoVox subscription at any time. However, please note the following:
- **Monthly Subscriptions**: Cancellation will be effective at the end of the current billing cycle. You will continue to have access until the period expires.
- **Annual Subscriptions**: Cancellation will be effective at the end of the subscription period. No partial refunds are provided for unused months.

### 2. Refund Policy
RepoVox offers refunds under the following conditions:
- **Billing Errors**: If you were charged incorrectly, contact support within **7 days** of the transaction.
- **Service Issues**: If RepoVox fails to function as promised and our support team is unable to resolve the issue within **14 days**, you may be eligible for a refund.
- **First-Time Users**: If you cancel within the first **7 days** of subscribing, you may request a full refund.

### 3. Non-Refundable Cases
Refunds will not be provided in the following scenarios:
- Change of mind after subscription.
- Failure to cancel before the renewal date.
- Partial months or unused service periods.
- Violation of our Terms of Service.

### 4. How to Request a Refund or Cancellation
To request a refund or cancel your subscription, follow these steps:

1. **Log in** to your RepoVox account.
2. Navigate to **Settings > Billing**.
3. Click on **Manage Subscription** and select **Cancel Subscription**.
4. To request a refund, email **[repovox.official@gmail.com](mailto:repovox.official@gmail.com)** with:
   - Your registered email address.
   - The reason for your refund request.
   - Any relevant transaction details.
